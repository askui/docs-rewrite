AskUI Desktop and the CLI sign in with your AskUI account. Your account belongs
to a **workspace** — the unit that owns your access tokens, usage, and billing.
You manage all of this in the **AskUI Hub**.

## Create your account

<Steps>

1. **Sign up** at the AskUI Hub with your email.

2. **Accept the terms and privacy policy.** You can't continue without
   consenting — this is a one-time step.

3. **Verify your email.** We send a verification link; open it to activate your
   account. (You can resend it from the prompt if it doesn't arrive.)

4. **Land in the Hub.** The Hub is onboarding-first — it guides you through
   setup, then becomes your reference for **tokens** and **settings**.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Authentication uses Auth0 (OIDC). The same login works across AskUI Desktop,
the CLI, and the Hub.
</Aside>
